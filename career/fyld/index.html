Fyld Inc

Fyld Inc. is a start-up that aims to provide its users with a mail witnessing service.

My entry as a Full-Stack developer started with a turnaround in the whole code architecture. I developed the migration from a monolithic architecture to a microservices architecture.

Being a mail witnessing service, the priority is to back up the information sent in the mails of the sending users. It works in such a way that a sender user sends an email to the original recipients and additionally adds Fyld among its recipients. When receiving the email, automatically gets all the information of news updated to the date and time corresponding to the arrival of the mail, in addition to obtaining the status and transactions of cryptocurrencies such as BTC, ETH, BCH, etc… And it is stored in a database with an architecture similar to a BlockChain.
